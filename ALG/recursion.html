Welcome to recursion.html! You have 5 options:

  1. isEven(number) to find if a number is even or odd.
  2. doubleArray(array) to double each element in the array.
  3. reverse(string) to reverse the string.
  4. triangle(nth) to find the nth triangular number.
  5. separate(string, separator) to separate the string based on a separator.
